About

Arewa women lack a way to track the data of what they spend and earn on a day-to-day basis and so lack the power to make better financial decisions. This puts them at a disadvantage as banks and other loan institutions lack appropriate data with which to create financial products and capital for business growth.

Kasafin Kudi is a web-based application that helps Arewa women track their expenditure and revenue in real-time while using insights developed from the data to empower more women to form new financial intelligence habits & give business owners access to more capital to expand their businesses.
